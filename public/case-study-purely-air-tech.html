The business

Purely Air Tech Ltd are an integrated mechanical services company based at Milton Park, Abingdon, Oxfordshire. They specialise in HVAC design and installation, VRF systems, compressed air and ventilation for commercial laboratories and offices across the UK. Founded in 2016, they are a Daikin D1 Business Partner with over 35 years of combined experience.

Their project portfolio includes work for Dyson, Air Canada Cargo at Heathrow, and biotech companies at Milton Park. They operate entirely on referral — no advertising, no cold outreach.

The problem

Despite an established reputation and high-profile clients, Purely Air Tech were invisible to AI search. When potential clients — or their referrers — verified them using AI tools like ChatGPT or Google Gemini, Purely Air Tech did not appear. No schema markup. No llms.txt. No meta description. AI engines had no way to identify, classify or recommend them.

A referral-based business depends on trust. If someone is referred to you and searches to verify who you are, AI giving no result is the same as a bad result.

What we did

Using Aenima, we generated and installed the complete AI visibility package in a single session.

  1. LocalBusiness schema — generated with full service catalogue, knowsAbout signals for each service, sameAs references, priceRange and dateModified. Installed into the site head via Breakdance Global Code.
  2. FAQPage schema — six targeted questions written specifically for PAT covering services, building types, location, experience, Daikin accreditation and how to make contact. Installed alongside the LocalBusiness schema.
  3. llms.txt — a machine-readable identity file summarising the business, services, who they work with, service areas and key credentials. Uploaded to the site root via 20i file manager.
  4. Meta description — written to be factual and AI-extractable. Added via Rank Math on the homepage.

Total time from first check to 100/100: one session. No developer required. No code editing. Files generated by Aenima, installed by copy-and-paste and file upload.
