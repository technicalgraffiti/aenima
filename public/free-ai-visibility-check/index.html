What the free check covers

Aenima runs nine real checks against your live domain — the same signals AI engines use when they crawl and index your site. Every check is live, every result is based on what's actually there right now.

01

Schema markup

Does your site have structured JSON-LD data telling AI your business name, type and location?

02

llms.txt

Is there a machine-readable identity file at your domain root for AI engines to read directly?

03

AI Crawler Access

Are GPTBot, ClaudeBot and PerplexityBot allowed to crawl your site in robots.txt?

04

Security & Trust

Is your site accessible over HTTPS and returning a valid HTTP 200 response?

05

Meta description

Does your homepage have a meta description AI engines can use as a text summary?

06

FAQ Schema

Is there structured FAQ data AI engines can quote directly in their answers?

07

OpenGraph tags

Are your social sharing tags complete — title, description and image?

08

Companies House

Is your UK government registration record linked in your schema? (Advisory — Ltd cos)

09

sameAs links

Are cross-platform identity links in your schema? (Advisory — strengthens AI trust)

Why nine checks? Because AI engines look at all nine. A site that passes seven but fails two critical ones can still be completely invisible to AI recommendations. You need the full picture.

What happens after the check

You get a score out of 100 with a plain English breakdown of every issue found — what it is, why it matters, and what priority it is. Critical issues are the ones blocking AI from identifying your business. High issues reduce your score and your chances of appearing in AI answers. Advisory items are improvements worth making when you can.

The free check also generates a downloadable PDF report — your Machine-Visibility Score — which you can keep on file or share with your web developer.

To fix the issues, Aenima generates the files your site needs — Schema markup, llms.txt and robots.txt AI permissions — from your business details. From £5.50 a month, no contract, cancel any time.

Who is the free check for?

Any UK business with a website. Trades, professional services, healthcare, retail, hospitality — if customers could find you through an AI recommendation and you're not sure if they can, the check will tell you in 60 seconds.

The check is especially useful if you've recently had a website built or redesigned, if you've changed your business name or address, or if you've never heard of GEO, AEO or llms.txt before now.
