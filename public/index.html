Cookie & Storage Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This policy explains how Aenima uses browser storage and what data is stored where.

1. What we use

Aenima does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies or third-party analytics cookies. We use browser localStorage — a standard web technology that stores data on your device only. It is not transmitted to third parties and is not accessible by other websites.

2. What is stored in localStorage

  • brd_u — your account details (name, email, plan, business info) to keep you logged in
  • brd_score — your most recent score result so it persists between sessions
  • brd_consent — a record of whether you have accepted this policy

This data never leaves your device unless you are logged in to a paid plan, in which case your account data is also stored securely in our database (Supabase — EU region).

3. Essential vs non-essential

All storage listed above is essential to the operation of the service — login, score results and consent records. Under the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025 and UK PECR, essential functional storage does not require prior consent. However we display this notice in the interest of full transparency.

4. Third-party storage

  • Stripe — sets its own cookies on the checkout page for fraud prevention. These are covered by Stripe’s own Privacy Policy.

5. How to clear your data

To remove all Aenima data from your browser, clear your browser's localStorage. In Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files. This will log you out and remove your saved score.

6. Changes to this policy

We will notify registered users by email of any material changes. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
